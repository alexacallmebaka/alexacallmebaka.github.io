Lately, many people have been asking me the question, "How did you learn to do X thing"? Initially, this was a hard question for me to answer... how did I learn how to do that?! There are a lot of things I certainly didn't learn in school or from an online course.

This Fall, I was given the honor of giving one of ACM@KU's lightning talks. For my talk, I wanted to give an overview of the various resources, philosophies, people, and experiences that have taught me everything I know and fed my passion for programming outside the classroom.
